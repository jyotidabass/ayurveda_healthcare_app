{% blocktrans %} Rasāyu Cancer Clinic (RCC) is a unique pioneering venture which offers comprehensive solutions to cancer patients. {% blocktrans %} Dyumna women's clinic (DWC) is focused on preventive as well as curative aspects pertaining to women's health. This is the brainchild of Dr. Vineeta Bendale under the umbrella of Rasāyu Āyurveda Clinics. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Āyurveda Rasayani (Manufacturing Unit) {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Dr. Bendale was passionate about Āyurveda medicine manufacturing initially at personal scale. In the year 2000, Dr. Bendale set up an Indian FDA approved Āyurveda drugs manufacturing unit near Pune. He got a lot of support from various Āyurveda practitioners and experts from all over India. Dr. Bendale has ensured that the manufacturing processes and ingredients strictly adhere to original Āyurveda literature and its compliance, ensuring authentic drugs. Over the years, it has transformed into a unique, state-of-the-art manufacturing unit in Mahalunge –Pune spread in about 75000 Sqft. land area. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Rasayani Biologics (Fundamental Drug Research Centre) - DSIR approved lab {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} An innovation driven biotech integrating wisdom of Āyurveda with modern day research and regulatory compliance, RBPL was founded in 2007 in Pune. It specializes at developing proprietary oral therapeutics for oncology and chronic diseases. The proprietary products contain noble metals and base elements processed using a patented green technology. RBPL has a well equipped research facility, recognized by Department of Science and Industrial Research (DSIR) and funded by the Department of Biotechnology (DBT), Govt. of India and a pilot manufacturing facility for active Pharmaceutical ingredients (API) approved by Indian FDA. {% endblocktrans %}
