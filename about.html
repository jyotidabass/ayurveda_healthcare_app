{% blocktrans %} Āyurveda is an ancient Indian science practiced since millenia. Āyurveda inherently focuses on preventive healthcare. This app attempts to do a high level health and risk assessment through interactive questionnaires and tracking and give personalised and preventive healthcare recommendations. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Wellness (svāsthya) assessment" %}

{% blocktrans %} Āyurveda classifies a normally healthy person as disease-free (nirogī) and truly healthy (svastha). A svastha person is more likely to enjoy a happier, longer and fulfilling life. The app attempts to do a high level health assessment of the user based on a health assessment questionnaire. The app provides recommendations to improve or maintain a good health. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Prakṛti assessment" %}

{% blocktrans %} The life style as recommended by Āyurveda is quite easy to follow. In India, the general daily and seasonal routines and regimens prescribed by Āyurveda were quite well known and practiced by the people and was traditionally passed on through generations. However, due to various factors this knowledge and practice has gradually waned. This app aims to enable users to inculcate this practice and follow it throughout their life. Based on the user’s prakṛti (constitutional analysis) assessment, the app will give personalized and timely recommendations on daily and seasonal routine and regimen (dinacaryā, ṛtucaryā). {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Risk assessment and mitigation" %}

{% blocktrans %} Based on a person's quality of tissue (dhātu sāratā), family history, lifestyle, and other factors, Āyurveda enables assessing health risks and accordingly recommending preventive measures for the same. {% endblocktrans %}
